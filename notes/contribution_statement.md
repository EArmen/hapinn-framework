# Contribution Statement

This repository supports the manuscript:

**HAPINN: A Solver-Guided Anchor-Based Framework for Parameterized Differential Equations**

## Nature of the contribution

The contribution of the associated article is primarily:

- conceptual,
- methodological,
- theoretical.

The manuscript does not present a new large-scale benchmark study. Instead, it formalizes and analyzes a solver-guided framework for parameterized differential equations.

## Main scientific contributions

The paper contributes the following:

1. A unified abstract formulation of HAPINN for parameterized PDE problems.
2. A canonical hybrid loss structure combining residual terms and anchor consistency.
3. A projection-based transfer theorem in a coercive elliptic model setting.
4. Finite-dimensional sufficient conditions for anchor-residual stability.
5. An analytical toy model clarifying the effect of anchors.
6. A five-component decomposition of the total approximation error.
7. A framework-level interpretation of a previously published hybrid PINN--TCAD semiconductor realization.

## Why this repository exists

This repository is intended to preserve the article source, ensure transparency, and document the actual scope of the work without overstating its empirical content.
