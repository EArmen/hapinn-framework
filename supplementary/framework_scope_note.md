# Framework Scope Note

This note clarifies the scope of the HAPINN manuscript and the repository.

## What the paper does

The paper:

- introduces a unified framework;
- gives a mathematical interpretation of solver-generated anchors;
- develops a projection-based theoretical skeleton in a coercive elliptic setting;
- provides an analytical toy model;
- interprets a previously published semiconductor realization as a concrete instance.

## What the paper does not do

The paper does not:

- claim a new large benchmark campaign;
- claim a new universal adaptive weighting rule;
- claim a complete nonlinear convergence theory for neural network parameterizations;
- claim that the prior semiconductor results are newly generated in this manuscript.

## Why this matters

This clarification is important both for academic honesty and for proper interpretation of the paper as a framework contribution rather than as a benchmark-driven empirical article.
